Abhangas for Lord Vitthal

Welcome to a divine collection of Abhangas, devotional songs that celebrate the glory of Lord Vitthal. These timeless compositions by revered saints such as Sant Tukaram, Sant Dnyaneshwar, and others inspire faith and devotion. Listen to the melodious chants, immerse yourself in devotion, and find peace through the sacred lyrics.
